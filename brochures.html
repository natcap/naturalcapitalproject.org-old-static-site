Scenarios for InVEST

Scenarios are essentially visions of alternative futures, which are frequently necessary for modeling changes in ecosystem services under different policy efforts. These publications go over best practices for how to create and use scenarios for your own work, explain how NatCap uses scenarios, and covers several case studies of how we have used scenarios in different situations.

Summaries of how scenarios are used in InVEST, suggestions for their effective use, and examples of their utilization.
