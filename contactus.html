InVEST Users

InVEST users have a variety of resources available to them to help use the InVEST suite of software efficiently and effectively. A complete list of online resources available to InVEST users can be found on the InVEST page of our website. If you have a specific question about InVEST, we recomend you post it on the NatCap Forums, and one of our model developers or software engineers will get back to you shortly with an answer.


Collaboration Requests

Organizations wishing to submit a formal request for collaboration with the Natural Capital Project should email invest@naturalcapitalproject.org with a short description of your organization and the project you would like to collaborate on. Please include if and how you intend to use the InVEST software suite, what environmental or social questions the project is attempting to answer, and an approximate timeline for the project, if available. We will do our best to follow up on your request and get back to you in a timely manner.
