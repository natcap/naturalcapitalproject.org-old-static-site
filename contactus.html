Donations: Thank you for your interest in donating to the Natural Capital Project. If you would like to make a donation, please mail a check to the address listed above. If you have any questions about donations please feel free to email our Finance, Grants & Operations Manager, Gail Kaiser, at gail.kaiser@stanford.edu.

Staff Contact Information: If you are interested in contacting a particular individual at the Natural Capital Project, please visit our staff page for contact information for all of our full-time staff.

Jobs: All current job openings at the natural capital project our listed on our jobs page. Any future job openings will be posted on our website and announced on Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn.

InVEST Users: InVEST users have a variety of resources available to them to help use the InVEST suite of software efficiently and effectively. A complete list of online resources available to InVEST users can be found on the InVEST page of our website. If you have a specific question about InVEST that was not answered by one of these other services, email your question directly to our Lead Software Developer, Rich Sharp, at richsharp@stanford.edu.

Collaboration Requests: Organizations wishing to submit a formal request for collaboration with the Natural Capital Project should email invest@naturalcapitalproject.org with a short description of your organization and the project you would like to collaborate on. Please include if and how you intend to use the InVEST software suite, what environmental or social questions the project is attempting to answer, and an approximate timeline for the project, if available. We will do our best to follow up on your request and get back to you in a timely manner.

Press: Press and media inquires can be made though our Communications Manager, Elizabeth Rauer, at elizabeth.rauer@stanford.edu or (650) 724-3108.
