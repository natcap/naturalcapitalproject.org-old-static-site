Chuck Katz
Charles Katz, Jr. is acting as an advisor on several of the Natural Capital Project strategic initiatives.  Chuck practiced law for 25 years in corporate finance at Perkins Coie, Seattle, Washington, and then served as Executive Vice President of Opsware, Inc., a Sunnyvale, California information technologies software company.  Currently he serves on the board of outdoor retailer REI, Inc. and has been active with the Student Conservation Association, the World Wildlife Fund and other conservation organizations.  He is a member of the Board of Advisors of the Woods Institute for the Environment and Stanford's School of Earth Sciences.  Chuck holds an AB degree from Stanford University, an MA degree from New York University and a JD degree from the University of Texas.
