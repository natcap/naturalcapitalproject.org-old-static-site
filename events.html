InVEST Training - Latin America

October 23-35, 2012
Mexico City, Mexico

The Natural Capital Project held an InVEST training in Mexico City with Comisión Nacional de Áreas Naturales Protegidas (CONANP). The training covered our tools and approaches to ecosystem service valuation, including how to use the Integrated Valuation of Environmental Services and Tradeoffs (InVEST) software suite.
