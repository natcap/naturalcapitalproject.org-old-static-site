Upcoming Events & InVEST Trainings


InVEST Training - Latin America

October 23-35, 2012
Mexico City, Mexico
Cost: $300

The Natural Capital Project is having an InVEST training in Mexico City with Comisión Nacional de Áreas Naturales Protegidas (CONANP). The training will cover our tools and approaches to ecosystem service valuation, including how to use the Integrated Valuation of Environmental Services and Tradeoffs (InVEST) software suite.


InVEST Training - Europe

November 5-9, 2012
Lisbon, Portugal

Information is now available online from our host institution, Instituto Superior Tecnico, on this InVEST training, including course information and how to formally register for the training. This one week course will cover nearly all of our marine and terrestrial InVEST models, as well as tips and techniques on how best to deploy our tools and approaches in the field. To learn more visit: ecosystemservicescourse2012.org


InVEST Training - Stanford University

RIOS & InVEST Training - Latin America

Spring, 2013
Latin America

Our Resource Investment Optimization System (RIOS) water funds tool should be publicly released sometime in the spring of 2013. The official release of the software program will be accompanied by an RIOS and InVEST training to be held somewhere in Latin America.
