NatCap Annual Meeting & InVEST Training - Stanford University

March 13th - 15th, 2013
Stanford, CA
Cost: $300
More information and registration

The Natural Capital Project will be holding our 2nd Annual Meeting & InVEST Training at Stanford University in Palo Alto, CA from March 13th-15th, 2013. The meeting will provide an opportunity for InVEST users and others interested in ecosystem services mapping and valuation, to learn more about the science and practice of using an ecosystem services approach and tools in real decisions. Attendees can get hands-on experience with NatCap tools, and network and share stories about their work. The training will include how to run and use all of the models in the Integrated Valuation of Environmental Services and Tradeoffs (InVEST) software suite, as well as presentations by users on how our tools and approaches are being used in decision contexts around the world. Last year, over 100 people participated in our inaugural annual meeting and InVEST training, and this year we anticipate an even greater turnout. There is a small fee to attend the training, with discounts available to people working in one of our partner institutions, and scholarships available for those in need.

Registration is now open, so sign up soon!



Natural Capital Project InVEST Training

June 12-14, 2013
Burlington, VT
Cost: $600, $300 for early registration
More information and registration

The Natural Capital Project (NatCap) cordially invites you to attend our InVEST Training in Vermont on June 13th and 14th, 2013. This two-day course will teach participants how to use the InVEST software suite and ways to apply approaches and tools for ecosystem service valuation in decision-making. A preliminary program with an introduction to InVEST and the natural capital approach will be held on June 12 at the United States Society for Ecological Economics conference (http://www.uvm.edu/conferences/ussee), which all registrants are encouraged to attend.

Registration is open at a reduced rate until May 1st.
