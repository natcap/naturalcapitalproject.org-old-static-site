InVEST Models


Integrated Valuation of Environmental Services and Tradeoffs (InVEST) is a free and open-source software suite developed by the Natural Capital Project to inform and improve natural resource management and investment decisions. InVEST maps and values the goods and services from nature that contribute to sustaining and fulfilling human life.

InVEST's modular toolset enables users to quantify, visualize and compare the delivery of key ecosystem services under different scenarios of land, water, and marine uses. InVEST model outputs describe natural resources in terms of their biophysical supply, the service they provide humans, or their projected socioeconomic value. These outputs provide a framework for public and private enterprises, land managers and other decision-makers to evaluate the effects their decisions will have on the landscape and on human well-being.


Models

InVEST currently includes 15 models that analyze different aspects of marine and terrestrial environments:

Aesthetic Quality
Models and maps the visibility of features on a seascape or landscape

Biodiversity
Models and maps habitat quality and habitat loss

Carbon
Models and values carbon storage and sequestration on land

Coastal Protection
Values the benefits of nearshore habitats for coastal protection

Coastal Vulnerability
Assesses the risk to coastal areas from storms

Crop Pollination
Assesses the value of wild bees to agriculture across a landscape

Habitat Risk Assessment
Evaluates the risk to marine or terrestrial habitats from anthropogenic factors

Managed Timber Production
Values timber harvest

Marine Fish Aquaculture
Estimates the harvest weight and value of farmed salmon

Marine Water Quality
Models concentration of pollutants at sea

Overlap Analysis
Identifies areas of potential conflict between various human uses

Reservoir Hydropower Production
Estimates the amount and value of hydropower produced by a reservoir

Sediment Retention
Maps soil loss and retention across a landscape

Water Purification
Maps nutrient retention across a landscape, and the value of avoided water treatment

Wave Energy
Models and values harvested energy from wave power facilities

User's Guide

The InVEST User's Guide presents in-depth information on how to use InVEST, the theory behind the models, common usage, required data inputs, limitations of the models, and a variety of other useful information.

InVEST training courses are offered several times a year in various locations around the world. Sign up for our newsletter to receive the latest information on our trainings, or see our schedule of upcoming trainings to decide which works best for you.

If you can't make it to a training, InVEST training videos presented by staff at the Natural Capital Project are the perfect way to get started using the models on your own.
