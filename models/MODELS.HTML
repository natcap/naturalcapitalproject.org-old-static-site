InVEST Models


Integrated Valuation of Environmental Services and Tradeoffs (InVEST) is a free and open-source software suite developed by the Natural Capital Project. It maps and values the goods and services from nature that contribute to sustaining and fulfilling human well being.


Models

These are the models currently available in InVEST:

  • Aesthetic Quality
  • Biodiversity
  • Carbon
  • Coastal Protection
  • Coastal Vulnerability

InVEST Documentation

The InVEST User's Guide has in-depth information on how to use the models, what goes into them, common usage, and limitations.

Training videos are available on all of our models so that you can participate in an InVEST training from the comfort of your own home.

The Natural Capital Project conducts InVEST trainings several times a year, in various locations around the globe. Sign up for our newsletter to recieve the latest information on our trainings, or see our schedule of upcoming trainings to see what InVEST trainings might work for you.
