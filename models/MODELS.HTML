InVEST Models


Integrated Valuation of Environmental Services and Tradeoffs (InVEST) is a free and open-source software suite developed by the Natural Capital Project. It maps and values the goods and services from nature that contribute to sustaining and fulfilling human well being.


Models

Aesthetic Quality - Models and maps the visability of new features on a seascape

Biodiversity - Models and maps habitat quality and rarity

Carbon - Models and values carbon storage and sequestration on land

Coastal Protection - Values the bennefits of nearshore habitats for coastal protection

Coastal Vulnerability - Asesses the risk of coastal areas from storm waves and surge

Crop Pollination - Assesses the value of wild bees to agriculture across a landscape

Habitat Risk Assessment - Evaluates the risk to coastal marine habitats from anthropogenic factors

Managed Timber Production - Values timber harvest

Marine Fish Aquaculture - Estimates the harvest weight and value of farmed salmon

Marine Water Quality - Models concentration of pollutants at sea

Reservoir Hydropower Production - Estimates the amount and value of hydropower produced by a reservoir

Overlap Analysis - Identifies areas of potential conflict between various human uses

Sediment Retention - Maps soil loss and rentention across a landscape

Water Purification - Maps nutrient retntion across a landscape, and the value of avoided water treatment

Wave Energy - Models and values harvested energy from wave power facilities

User's Guide

The InVEST User's Guide has in-depth information on how to use the models, what goes into them, common usage, and limitations.

Training videos are available on all of our models so that you can participate in an InVEST training from the comfort of your own home.

The Natural Capital Project conducts InVEST trainings several times a year, in various locations around the globe. Sign up for our newsletter to recieve the latest information on our trainings, or see our schedule of upcoming trainings to see what InVEST trainings might work for you.
