Scenario Generator Tool


The Scenario Generator tool provides a relatively simple method of generating scenarios based on land suitability. Scenarios describe a possible future and reflect important and uncertain future developments or choices. In order to be useful and meaningful to the decision-making process, scenarios need to be plausible, internally consistent, and relevant to the questions being addressed. Scenario development can be a complicated process and usually requires significant stakeholder input. The Scenario Generator tool helps to automate this process, quickly and easily translating stakeholders' visions of alternative futures into map form. It works on the principle that changes on land occur in areas that are relatively more suitable. Combining stakeholder input of transition likelihood with physical factors that determine suitability, the tool generates simple maps that depict future land cover.
