Aesthetic Quality Model


The InVEST Aesthetic Quality model employs viewshed analysis to estimate the visibility of new nearshore or offshore features. The model generates maps that can identify the visual footprint of offshore development plans and highlight coastal areas that are more likely to be directly affected by additions to the seascape. Coastal planners can incorporate these measures of visual impact into policy deliberations and development proposals to maximize the benefits of scenic views provided to local communities.


Applications

We have used the Aesthetic Quality Model in the following demonstration sites:

  • Coastal and Marine Spatial Planning on the West Coast of Vancouver Island
