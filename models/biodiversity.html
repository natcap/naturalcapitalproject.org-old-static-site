Biodiversity Model


The InVEST Biodiversity model uses habitat quality and rarity as proxies to analyze the biodiversity of a landscape. The model combines maps of land use land cover (LULC) with data on threats to biodiversity and habitat response in order to estimate the extent of habitat and vegetation types, and their states of degradation. Modeling biodiversity alongside environmental services enables users to compare spatial patterns and identify areas where conservation will most benefit natural systems and protect threatened species.
