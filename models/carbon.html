Carbon Sequestration Model


The InVEST Carbon Storage and Sequestration model estimates the current amount of carbon stored in a landscape and the amount it stores over time. Its use of land use and land cover (LULC) maps and data on wood harvest, harvested product degradation rates, and carbon pool stocks can inform understanding of how land use transitions impact the release of carbon into the atmosphere. The model can quantify the amount of sequestered carbon and value this environmental service by using additional data on the market value of carbon, its annual rate of change, and a discount rate.
