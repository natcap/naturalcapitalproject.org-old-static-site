The Natural Capital Project Ten Year Anniversary
Symposium and Celebration


Tuesday, October 13, 2015 at 3 p.m.
Oberndorf Event Center, Stanford Graduate School of Business


Celebrating ten years of The Natural Capital Project's impact — past, present, and future — the symposium features two one-hour panels, starting at 3 p.m. The first panel will address investments in natural capital by corporate and government leaders, including cases in China, Myanmar, and Mozambique. The second panel considers frontiers in ecosystem services, such as how to improve urban livability, opportunities for new academic research, working with development banks as they green their investment portfolios, and how technology start-ups are pursuing natural capital.
