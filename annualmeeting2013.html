NatCap Annual Meeting & InVEST Training


Stanford University

March 13th - 15th, 2013
Stanford, CA

The Natural Capital Project held our Annual Meeting & InVEST Training at Stanford, March 13-15, 2013. It covered our tools and approaches to ecosystem service valuation, including how to use the Integrated Valuation of Environmental Services and Tradeoffs (InVEST) software suite, and provided a forum for users to network and share stories of their work on ecosystem services.
