Spain InVEST Training

May 27, 2014 - May 31, 2014
Vigo, Spain

The demand is growing for applications of natural capital information in decision-making. At the Spain InVEST Training, learn about how mapping and modelling ecosystem services (benefits people obtain from nature) can help uncover hidden costs and benefits of different natural resource management options, providing key information to improve the relationship between humans and the ecosystems on which we depend.

There will be an Introductory and Advanced course offered during this training. The one-day Introductory course, Decision with a Natural Capital Approach, will include lectures, facilitated discussion, and example case studies to highlight how natural capital and ecosystem services information can be incorporated into decision-making. The Advanced course, Training Workshop: Ecosystem Assessments that Matter, will take place over the following 3 1/2 days and will provide hands-on exercises in addition to lectures and facilitated discussion. Attendees will have the opportunity to learn and practice the different steps of an approach to incorporate natural capital and ecosystem service information in decisions.
