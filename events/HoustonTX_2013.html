Coastal Resilience and Ecosystem Services


Training Workshop

June 20, 2013 - June 21, 2013
Rice University | Houston, TX

NatCap collaborated with TNC to hold a one and half day training course on coastal resilience and ecosystem services models. The course was designed for participants working on coastal issues in the Gulf of Mexico and hosted at Rice University.


Highlights

1. Shared lessons learned from our approach to implementing coastal resilience and ecosystem service assessments to inform decision-making and increase the resilience of coastal communities in the Gulf region.

2. Introduced the InVEST ecosystem service accounting tool, its capabilities and limitations. We also discussed other models that can be linked to InVEST.

3. Explored the application of InVEST to design conservation areas, prioritize restoration activities, inform permitting processes, conduct marine spatial planning and explore climate adaptation strategies and plans.
