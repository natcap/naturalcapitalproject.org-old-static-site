InVEST and RIOS Training for TNC Eastern Division

May 29-30, 2014
Boston, MA

NatCap held a 2 day training for The Nature Conservancy's Eastern Division to teach TNC staff how to use InVEST and RIOS software. This specialized technical training was specifically designed to be relevant to projects The Nature Conservancy is involved in on the East Coast of the United States. TNC staff worked with NatCap to develop 3 case studies to cover during this training: land use and nutrient loading in New York State, catalyzing the protection of remnant floodplain forests d by valuing the multiple benefits they provide in the Connecticut River watershed, or land use practices and nutrient loading in the Cape Fear watershed.


Presentations

Intro to NatCap models, focusing on applications

Introduction to InVEST models – Theory and key concepts

InVEST spatial data requirements, sources, and pre-processing

Additional data requirements, sources, calibration – Adrian

Working with soils data and deriving model inputs

Targeting Investments in watersheds: Introduction to RIOS model, data and sources

Synthesizing and communicating results

New frontiers for NatCap models
