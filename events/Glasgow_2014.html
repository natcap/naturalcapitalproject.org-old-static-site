Marine Spatial Planning Tools Workshop

August 17, 2014
Glasgow, Scotland

At the International Marine Conservation Congress (IMCC), members of the Natural Capital Project hosted a workshop to demonstrate and receive feedback on our marine-tailored tools. Because this workshop was given as a part of the IMCC, attendence was limited to registered Congress participants.
