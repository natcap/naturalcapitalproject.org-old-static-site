Training Workshop on Ecosystem Service Benefits of Targeted Watershed Investments

May 21-23, 2014
Shimla, Himachal Pradesh, India

NatCap conducted a 3-day workshop and technical training with government staff from Himachal Pradesh. The goals of the workshop were to present the results of an assessment of ecosystem services for hydropower, and to build capacity on applying the RIOS and InVEST models for prioritizing watershed management activities and designing a state-level payments for ecosystem services scheme. Additionally, we discussed the steps needed to integrate these models into the development of natural capital accounts.
