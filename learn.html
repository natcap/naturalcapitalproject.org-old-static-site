Learn

Have questions about InVEST or RIOS? Do you wish to learn more about accounting for ecosystem services and NatCap's approach? From our user help forum to our trainings and online courses, gain knowledge and training to effectively use our tools and learn more about natural capital approaches and how to use ecosystem service information to make more informed decisions.



User Help Forum

Have questions or need help with our tools? Check out NatCap Forums where you can chat with the InVEST and RIOS community, as well as receive help from our software engineers and scientists.



Natural Capital Symposium

At the Natural Capital Symposium, learn about NatCap's approach to supporting decisions through ecosystem service mapping, modeling, and valuation, attend hands-on sessions with InVEST and RIOS, share stories of your work in the field, and help to build a community of practice.



InVEST Trainings

NatCap hosts a number of introductory and technical trainings around the globe. Get details on where and when our upcoming trainings are, and how to register.



Online Course (MOOC)

Interested in how natural capital approaches can inform decisions taken by governments, multi-lateral development institutions, the private and finance sectors, and non-governmental organizations? Register for our online course NCP-101 to learn more about our approach to using ecosystem service information to inform decisions.

Our friends and collaborators at the Stockholm Resilience Center have also recently launch a MOOC, Planetary Boundaries and Human Opportunities, which we highly reccommend.

Fellows Program
