Incorporating Ecosystem Services into Decisions

The Natural Capital project is working to include natural capital and ecosystem services in decisions that impact the environment, and is doing so in a variety of decision-contexts around the world. We have found the Integrated Valuation of Environmental Services and Tradeoffs (InVEST) to be an invaluable tool in including ecosystem services thinking into the decision-making process. InVEST can help provide a framework for governments, corporations, development banks, conservation organizations and other decision makers to evaluate the impacts of their decisions on the environment and on human well-being. Ecosystem service information can be particularly important to consider when making long-term planning decisions that determine how we will use our lands and waters. Used as part of NatCap's approach to engaging decision-makers on the ground, InVEST can provide the necessary information on relevant ecosystem services to decisions involving spatial planning, permitting or mitigation decisions, environmental impact assessments, climate adaptation planning, coastal hazard mitigation, corporate risk management, payment for ecosystem services, and restoration.
