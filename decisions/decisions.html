Incorporating Ecosystem Services into Decisions

The Natural Capital Project applies its ecosystem service modeling tools to inform a broad range of policy and planning decisions around the world.

The Integrated Valuation of Ecosystem Services and Tradeoffs (InVEST) software suite provides a framework for governments, corporations, development banks, conservation organizations and individuals to evaluate the impacts of their planning and development decisions on the environment and on human well-being.

Ecosystem service information is particularly important to consider in long-term planning efforts that determine the use lands and waters. InVEST provides information on ecosystem services relevant to spatial planning processes, permitting or mitigation decisions, environmental impact assessments, climate adaptation planning, coastal hazard mitigation, corporate risk management, payment for ecosystem services, and restoration planning.
